# Credit Risk Analysis — Shell PLC & BP plc

## Project Overview
End-to-end credit risk analysis project covering financial statement 
analysis, IFRS 9 ECL modelling, stress testing and credit decision 
making for Shell PLC and BP plc.

## What This Project Covers
- Power BI Credit Risk Dashboard
- Financial ratio analysis (2023-2025)
- IFRS 9 ECL staging and PD modelling
- Stress testing and risk scorecard
- Credit memorandum with facility enhancement decision
- Shell full sanction USD 800M
- BP partial sanction USD 465M (Watch List)

## Tools Used
- Power BI
- Microsoft Excel
- AI-assisted productivity

Credit Risk | IFRS 9 | Trade Finance | UCP 600 | 
Power BI | Financial Analysis | Corporate Banking | 
FCA & PRA Regulatory Considerations
